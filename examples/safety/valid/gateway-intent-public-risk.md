# Gateway Intent Public-Risk Fixture

This fixture models public-safe AO Mission gateway intent text.

- Telegram and A2A inputs are intent/readback only.
- Gateway intents do not approve policy.
- Gateway intents do not execute mutation.
- Gateway intents do not mutate repositories.
- Gateway intents do not call providers.
- Gateway intents do not publish releases.
- Gateway intents do not use credentials.
- Gateway intents continue through AO Mission routing, AO Atlas provenance, and
  AO Foundry gates.
